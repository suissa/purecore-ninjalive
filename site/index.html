Why ninjalive?

Total Anonymity

No account required. Just pick a name and join. Your identity remains yours.

Serverless Media

We use P2P Mesh technology. Video and audio go directly between you and your peers. We don't see it.

Military Encryption

Powered by WebRTC standard encryption (DTLS/SRTP). Secure by default.

Free & Open Source

Build your own meeting system.

ninjalive is 100% open source. You can clone the repository, modify it, and use it however you like, including for commercial projects.

The only condition is to keep the 'Powered by ninjalive' credit with a link to our original repository. We want to encourage the creation of secure meeting systems without heavy infrastructure!
