Ninja Features

Everything you need for productive and private meetings.

P2P Mesh Network

Direct connections between participants, ensuring low latency and maximum privacy.

Screen Sharing

Share presentations or your entire desktop with a single click, in high definition.

Real-time Chat

Exchange messages and links during the call. History is wiped as soon as the room closes.

Admin Powers

If you are the room creator, you get special powers: Mute All, Mute specific users, or Kick them out properly.

Audio Transcription

Auto-local transcription with an option to download the full session transcript as a .txt file.

Ninja AI Integration

Connect your own API key to unlock AI chat, real-time translation, advanced transcription, and automatic meeting summaries.

Why ninjalive?

Designed for the security-conscious.

Total Anonymity

No account required. Just pick a name and join. Your identity remains yours.

Serverless Media

We use P2P Mesh technology. Video and audio go directly between you and your peers. We don't see it.

Military Encryption

Powered by WebRTC standard encryption (DTLS/SRTP). Secure by default.

Free & Open Source

Build your own meeting system.

ninjalive is 100% open source. You can clone the repository, modify it, and use it however you like, including for commercial projects.

The only condition is to keep the 'Powered by ninjalive' credit with a link to our original repository. We want to encourage the creation of secure meeting systems without heavy infrastructure!
